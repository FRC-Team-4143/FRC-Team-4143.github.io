1. Go to Autodesk’s Education Portal
- Visit the [Autodesk Education Software Page](https://www.autodesk.com/education/edu-software/fusion)

2. Create an Autodesk Account
- Click “Get Started” or “Sign In” if you already have an account.
    - Use your school email address if possible—it helps with verification

3. Verify Your Educational Status
- Select your role - Student.
    - Choose  Mentor if you're a mentor working with a FIRST team.
- Provide your school or organization name and expected graduation date.
