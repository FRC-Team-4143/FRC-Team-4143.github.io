Giving parts decriptive names help your fellow designers to quickly and efficiently find parts and avoid repeat efforts.

### Onshape Parts

**Year_Subsystem_Partname**

**Example:**


- 4143-25-Robot-A
    - 4143-25-Intake_A_0000
        - 4143-25-Intake-P-0001
        - 4143-25-Intake-P-0002
        - 4143-25-Intake-P-0003
        - 4143-25-Intake-A-0100
            - 4143-25-Intake-P-0101 
            - 4143-25-Intake-P-0102 
    - 4143-25-Arm-A-0000
    - **etc**




**Example:**
- Dory
    - Intake_A_0000
        - Intake_0001
        - Intake_0002
        - Intake_0003
        - Intake_0100           *
            - Intake_0101
            - Intake_0102
    - 2025_Arm  
- **etc**

***Sub-Assemblies can have their own names. Parts inside these subassemblies do not include the subassemblie's name.**
